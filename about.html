Welcome to Our Journey

History

Founded in Onitsha in 2018, we started by booking authentic trips for friends across Nigeria. Guiding travelers from Lagos markets to Ghana’s coast showed us people wanted more than hotels — they wanted trust and local insight. That became our company.

Mission

To make African travel accessible, responsible, and personal. We use local partners, run small groups, and design trips that support communities while giving you real connection — not just photos
